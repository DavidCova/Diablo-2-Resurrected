|Result|Ingredients|

|*Upgrade Normal Set Armor to Exceptional*<br>Same iLvl & properties<br>Runes, jewels & gems retained<br>|1x Normal Set Armor+ 1 Tal Rune+ 1 Shael Rune+ 1x Perfect Diamond|
